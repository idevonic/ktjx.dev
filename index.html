About Me

My journey started in national service with the Singapore Civil Defence Force, where I worked with critical communication systems. Since then, I’ve explored enterprise networking at NCS and gained data centre exposure at Equinix.

Currently, I’m pursuing a Diploma in Infocomm Security Management at Singapore Polytechnic. Along the way, I’ve picked up hands-on experience with Cisco IOS, Windows Server, VLANs, and troubleshooting networks. What excites me most is solving real infrastructure challenges and making sure systems are both reliable and secure.

I’m curious, adaptable, and always eager to learn how technology can be applied to create stronger, safer digital environments.
