About Me

My journey began during national service with the Singapore Civil Defence Force, where I worked with critical communication systems. Since then, I’ve explored enterprise networking at NCS and gained data centre exposure at Equinix.

I’m currently pursuing a Diploma in Infocomm Security Management at Singapore Polytechnic. Along the way, I’ve built hands-on experience with Cisco IOS, Windows Server, VLANs, and network troubleshooting. What excites me most is tackling real infrastructure challenges and ensuring systems remain both reliable and secure.

I’m curious, adaptable, and always eager to learn how technology can be applied to build stronger, safer digital environments.
